HEALTH BENEFITS

While swimming, impact stress is take off of your body, providing relaxing sensations from an increasing heart rate. It will also lower blood pressure and increase bone strength.
Swimming can also be a good way of low-impact therapy for other injuries/conditions. Joint pain can also be reduced from swimming.
Swimming improves coordination, balance, posture, and flexibility. It also builds up endurance (from lap swimming) and lots of muscle strength.
