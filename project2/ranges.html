RANGES OF SWIMMING

STROKES: swimming has a variety of strokes to swim. you can choose from 4 strokes, which are butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke, and freestlye. the main stroke is freestyle, which you mostly do with longer swims. if you don't like swimming freestyle, you can learn breaststroke. if you don't like breaststroke, you can swim backstroke, etc.
DISTANCES: when you race in swimming, you have a choice of many many different events with different strokes and distances. it ranges from the 50 freestyle to the 200 backstroke to the 400im (individual medley- where you swim all the strokes) to the mile (1650 yards freestyle).
BENEFITS: there are health benefits (heart, lungs), physical benefits (toned muscles) and psychological benefits (mental health).
